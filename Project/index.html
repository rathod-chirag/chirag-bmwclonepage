BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a leading German luxury automotive manufacturer, known for its premium cars and motorcycles (BMW Motorrad), alongside brands like MINI and Rolls-Royce. Founded in 1916, it evolved from aircraft engines to cars, becoming famous for performance, engineering, and luxury, while also focusing heavily on sustainability and electric vehicle innovation. Headquartered in Munich, BMW is a global giant with production and sales worldwide, part of the "German Big 3" luxury makers.
